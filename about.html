How I became a web developer

My interest in programming started a few years ago while looking at my wife coding. I wanted to understand what was that weird looking screen with opened terminals and lines of, what was for me then, gibberish! I decided to learn Python but then I stumbled on what would become let’s say, my third love, Javascript! I got immediately hooked, the more I knew the more I wanted to know. It was like learning to play music all over again.

I started to practice this new skill to get my feet wet. As you may assume, I went through numerous courses on the main learning portals, cementing my knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript and the respective frameworks and libraries. I also took my first peek at back-end technologies (because let’s be honest, there is where the magic start to happen) with the goal of becoming a MERN stack developer.

Looking back to all that SASS, Bootstrap, jQuery, React, Node, Express, Mongo and whatnot I’m feeling my feet getting wet, yes, but also standing at the edge of an ocean and I can't wait to go sailing!
